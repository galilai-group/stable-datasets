MovingMNIST: the canonical 10K-sequence test set from Srivastava et al. (2015).

Moving MNIST (test split only).

10,000 sequences of 20 grayscale frames at 64x64. The canonical artifact has no class labels; training data is conventionally generated procedurally from MNIST digits, which is out of scope here.
